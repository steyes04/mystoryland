Company Overview

Built in Dubai. Trusted Across the UAE.

Established in 1998, Al Turath Al Aseel Contracting L.L.C. (ATC) has earned its place as a trusted leader in the UAE's construction and infrastructure sector. Headquartered in Dubai, ATC brings over two decades of proven experience delivering end-to-end solutions across civil construction, MEP, fit-out works and facility management — all executed with precision, reliability and innovation.

Our strength lies in an integrated, self-performing approach that allows us to manage projects entirely in-house — from structural works to complex MEP systems and architectural finishes. Whether delivering large-scale government developments or private sector ventures, ATC is known for completing projects on time, within budget, and above expectations.
